Consultancy can be a single, focused review — a few days spent understanding your system and returning a prioritized set of recommendations — or a standing relationship where we're available for the decisions that are expensive to get wrong.

Either way, the aim is the same: give you a clear, honest picture of where you are, where you're headed, and the most direct route between the two.
